Who is Pelé?

Edson Arantes do Nascimento, more commonly know by his nickname Pelé, was a Brazilian professional football player who played as a forward for the brazilian team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, he was among the most successful and popular sports figures of the 20th century
